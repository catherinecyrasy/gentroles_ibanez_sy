Cambugahay Falls



Cambugahay Falls is a popular tourist attraction located in Siquijor, Philippines. It is a three-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and natural beauty. The falls are known for their crystal-clear turquoise waters and picturesque setting, making them a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Here are some unique experiences and activities that Cambugahay Falls can offer to tourists:

1. Swimming and Cliff Jumping: The main activity at Cambugahay Falls is swimming in the refreshing natural pools. Each tier of the waterfall has a large swimming area where visitors can take a dip and enjoy the cool waters. Additionally, if you're feeling adventurous, you can try cliff jumping from the designated jumping points into the deep pools below.

2. Tarzan Swing: One of the highlights of Cambugahay Falls is the Tarzan Swing. You can grab hold of a sturdy vine and swing across the falls before plunging into the inviting water. It's a thrilling experience that adds an extra element of excitement to your visit.

3. Bamboo Rafting: Bamboo rafts are available for rent at the falls, allowing visitors to relax and float on the calm pools while enjoying the serene surroundings. It's a great way to unwind and appreciate the natural beauty of the area.

4. Natural Jacuzzi: Some sections of the falls have natural jacuzzi-like spots created by cascading water, which can provide a relaxing and invigorating massage-like experience. These pockets of swirling water offer a unique way to unwind and enjoy the soothing power of nature.

5. Picnicking and Relaxation: Cambugahay Falls provides a serene environment with shaded areas and picnic spots where you can enjoy a leisurely meal or simply relax with friends and family. It's a great place to escape the heat and immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature.

6. Photography and Nature Walks: The picturesque beauty of Cambugahay Falls makes it a photographer's paradise. You can capture stunning shots of the falls, lush vegetation, and the vibrant blue waters. Nature walks in the surrounding area also offer opportunities to explore the flora and fauna of the region.

Overall, Cambugahay Falls offers a unique and enchanting experience to tourists. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or an escape into nature, a visit to these falls in Siquijor can provide you with an unforgettable time.
